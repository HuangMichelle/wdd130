Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this Rafting Site is to provide each and every one of our valuable outdoor friends the convenience of finding valuble information regarding our water rafting services. This includes contact information, pricing information, photo galleries of past customers.. etc.

Audience

Who are the target customers? Describe their life (or business) situation (age, financial situation, interests, and so on)? The targeted customers will be grouped focus or individuals that are in college, corporate workers, or families that are wanting to explore and get away from the regular life and to explore the outdoors and just to have a great time. What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website? We will provide differnt packages that will be tailored to each group mentioned. There will be a package for those who are more adventerous and want maybe a more bumpy ride and those who want a more safe and secure smooth ride. Every group will be taken care of and offered different packaged options. They can expect to find general information of why we are different than other rafting services with experienced and licenced rafters to ensure the safefy of everyone. They will find contact information, payments, and a frequently asked question section to find guidance. Discount information will be incorporated as well as scheduling. How will my audience usually access my site (desktop, laptop, mobile device)? Our audience will be able to access this site through desktop, laptop, and their mobile device.
