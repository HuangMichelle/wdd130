Choose Your Adventure

Experience the thrill of river rafting with our exciting trips

Trip 1

Skykomish River, Index, Washington: Duration: Full day The Skykomish River, located near Index, Washington, offers thrilling white water rafting experiences. Join a guided day trip and paddle through Class III and IV rapids as you navigate the scenic stretches of this beautiful river.
Enjoy the rush of adrenaline as you tackle the challenging rapids and take in the lush Pacific Northwest scenery. Several reputable outfitters in the area provide guided rafting trips and all the necessary equipment for a safe and enjoyable adventure.

Trip 2

Wenatchee River, Leavenworth, Washington: Duration: Full day Head to Leavenworth, Washington, and experience the excitement of white water rafting on the Wenatchee River. With its Class III rapids, the Wenatchee River offers an ideal blend of fun and adventure for intermediate rafters. Enjoy the thrill of paddling through rapids, splashing through waves, and taking in the picturesque scenery of the Cascade Mountains. Many rafting companies in Leavenworth offer day trips with experienced guides, providing you with a memorable and safe white water rafting experience.

Trip 3

Tieton River, Yakima, Washington: Duration: Full day For a more adrenaline-pumping white water rafting adventure, consider a day trip to the Tieton River near Yakima, Washington. This river provides an exciting experience with its challenging Class III and IV rapids. Prepare to get wet and enjoy the rush as you navigate through the thrilling rapids and stunning canyon landscapes. Various outfitters in the area offer guided day trips, ensuring you have the necessary equipment and expert guidance to make the most of your Tieton River rafting experience.
